# Simple Location Manager

**Nexus Link:** [Simple Location Manager](https://www.nexusmods.com/cyberpunk2077/mods/26454)

Ever found a preem photo spot or a perfect testing ground, only to lose it forever? **Simple Location Manager** lets you save, organise, and share your favourite coordinates in Night City via the CET overlay.

Feel free to use this thread to share and record your own location export strings!

## Why use it?

* **Never lose a spot:** Instantly save your current position with a timestamp or custom name.
* **Teleport Instantly:** Enable "Lazy Mode" in settings to warp to any saved location.
* **Share with Chooms:** Copy specific spots or entire districts as a base64 string that can be imported by other users directly.
* **Navigation:** Set a custom map pin to any saved spot (great for Autodrive).
* **Modder Friendly:** Create location presets to include in your mods for users. Check out the [Preset Mod Author Guide](https://www.nexusmods.com/cyberpunk2077/articles/1960). Example: [Japantown North Verticality - SLM Preset Mod](https://www.nexusmods.com/cyberpunk2077/mods/26743)

## Key Features

* **Auto-Organised:** Locations are automatically sorted by District and Sub-district.
* **Custom Categories:** Create your own categories (e.g., "New Totally Preem Mod Location", "Photos") with custom icons.
* **AMM Support:** Import locations directly from Appearance Menu Mod json files.
* **Search:** Filter your list by name or district in real-time.
* **Quick Save Hotkey:** Bind a key in the CET Bindings menu to instantly save your location without opening the overlay.
* **Debug Tools:** Use **Print Coordinates** or **Dump District Info** in settings to output raw data to the CET console/log.

## How to use

1. Open the **CET Overlay**.
2. Click the **+** button in the SLM window to save your current spot.
3. Click the **Pin** icon to set a waypoint, or the **Teleport** icon (if enabled) to warp there.
4. **Right-click** any category or location to Export it to your clipboard.
